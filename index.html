About Me

It's my pleasure to introduce myself!

My name is Nicol Saha, and after almost a decade as an academic researcher, I decided to turn my world upside down and make passion my work. And here I am, designing websites, illustrating and taking photographs for clients. I couldn’t have imagined a better job. The best part? Sharing my passion with you!

I treat design with the greatest care, because we all know that first impressions last. By understanding where you are and where you want to go, I work together with you towards turning your branding ideas into market-ready solutions. So are you in need of a new website, or maybe just a redesign of a current one? As a front-end developer I can both design and code it completely according to your custom wants and needs!
